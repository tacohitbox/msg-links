Privacy

MsgLinks cares about your privacy. Absolutely no frontend Javascript is required to use our service, nor do we use any cookies or logging on our end when you access our web server. Below is how we use your data in our server.

# How your data is used

If you plan on not using the bot at all and if none of your messages are linked with MsgLinks - absolutely no data about you is served through the bot or stored on our end.

If you set any settings (including disabling any kind of messages) - your user ID and any input (like message ID) given to the bot is stored on our server privately.

When a message is served through MsgLinks - the messages data is not saved to our servers (unless it is cached), but any attachments/embeds (unless disabled) and the content of your message will be served through our server.

# How to prevent embedding

You can disable people linking your message, attachments, or any other data served through MsgLinks via our website or via bot commands (see here).
